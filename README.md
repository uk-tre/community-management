# 👋 Welcome to the UK TRE Community Management Working Repo 

## 💡Introduction

This repo is for storing everything related to Community Management for the UK TRE Community.

It is run and maintained by the [Community Management Working Group (CMWG)](https://www.uktre.org/en/latest/structure/community-management.html).

This repo is in general content light - unless not possible, all resources created within this working group will be openly published and shared on the website repo.

This repo is mainly for

- Tracking community issues and discussions
- Planning community events
- Tracking any other tasks that fall under the remit of the CMWG

For more information on the UK TRE Community, please visit [our website](https://www.uktre.org/en/latest/)

## 📣 Community issues and discussions

Any discussion for the community should be opened as an issue on this repository.
This includes:

- Ideas for community feedback
- Proposals for new Working Groups, community resources, events or governance documentation
- Any other community initiatives to be tracked.

Any issues related to updating website content should be opened on our [website repo](https://github.com/uk-tre/website).

To see how to open an issue following our community guidelines, please read our [Contribution guide](https://github.com/uk-tre/website/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) ✨

## 🤝Joining the Community Management Working Group

The CMWG is open for anyone to join!

We have weekly meetings on Tuesdays at 11am to discuss working group matters.

We share joining instructions on the `#community-management` channel on the [UK TRE Slack](https://join.slack.com/t/uktrecommunity/shared_invite/zt-2gep86apc-QMLyIdrC2oIIsxTRzLxUqA) ahead of each meeting.
